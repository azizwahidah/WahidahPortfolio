My Story

I saved $100K on a $45K salary. Then I started teaching other women how.

I'm a first-gen daughter of immigrants who grew up with money advice built around survival and guilt. Nobody taught me how money actually works — so I figured it out myself and built a system around it.

That system became the Invisible Money Method — a Two-Step Anti-Budget approach that prioritizes emergency funds, automation, and simplicity over traditional budgeting.

I help women stop tracking every dollar and start building real wealth — with systems that work even when life doesn't.

Now, through Wealth Wahidah, I create content that challenges outdated money rules and replaces them with strategies that actually fit the lives of first-gen women, millennials, and anyone who was never handed a financial playbook.
